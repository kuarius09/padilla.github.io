About Food in Sticks


"Turo-turo" street food

Welcome to Food in Sticks, our world of Filipino street food! We bring the vibrant flavors of the streets to your home, offering iconic snacks and dishes like isaw, kwek-kwek, fish balls, and more. Our goal is to share the unique experience of Filipino street food culture—delicious, affordable, and full of flavor. Whether you are craving a quick bite or a nostalgic meal, we make it easy to order your favorites online and enjoy them fresh and hot at your convenience. Perfect for casual get-togethers or just satisfying your cravings!

”Street food is the ultimate comfort food, reminding us that great flavors await at every corner.” - Alex Thompson
